Formulas:

BASICS:
mean=Σx/#of terms
mode:number that gets repeated the most
median(50th percentile)=the middle of the dataset(sorted)
standard deviation(sample)=sx=Σ(x-mean)^2/terms-1
standard deviation(population)=σ=Σ(x-mean)^2/terms
variance=(standard deviation)^2 || standard deviation=(variance)^1/2
range=max-min
interquartile range = q3-q1
outliers= 1.5*(interquartile range)+q3, q1-1.5*(interquartile range)

CONFIDENCE INTERVALS:
(z distribution )
x̄ ± z(critical)*[(standard deviation:σ)/terms^1/2]
(proportion)
p=p̂ ± z(critical)[(p̂(1-p̂))/n]^1/2
(t distribution)
x̄ ± t(critical)*[(standard deviation:S)/terms^1/2] df:n-1
2 - SAMPLE
HYPOTHESIS TESTING:
(one sample)
z-statistic=x̄-hypothesized value/(σ/terms)^1/2
t-statistic=x̄-hypothesized value/(S/terms)^1/2
proportion(z)=(p̂-p)/[p(1-p)/terms]
(two sample)
(z-distribution)
z-statistic=x̄(first mean)-x̄(second mean)-hypothesized value/[(σ1^2/terms of first sample)+(σ2^2/termsof second sample])
(t-distribution)
t-statistic=x̄(first mean)-x̄(second mean)-hypothesized value/[(S1^2/terms of first sample)+(S2^2/termsof second sample])
(proportion)
proportion(z)=(p̂1-p̂2-p1-p2)/(p(1-p)*(1/terms1+1/terms2))
chi-square Expected value:(total row)x(total column)/sample size
chi-square test:Σ(Observed value-Expected value)^2/Expected value

CORRELATION COEFFICIENT:
y=a+bx
r=(1/terms-1)*(Σ[(x-x̄)/s(x)*(y-ȳ)/s(y)])
slope(b)=terms*ΣXY-ΣX*ΣY/termsΣX^2-(ΣX)^2
